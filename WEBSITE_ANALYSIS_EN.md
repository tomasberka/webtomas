### ‚ö†Ô∏è What Needs Improvement

#### 1. **Too Broad Targeting** üéØ
**Problem:** The site addresses "e-shops, corporations, and personal brands" - too broad.

**Consequence:** Potential client thinks: *"It's for everyone = it's not specifically for me."*

**Solution:**
- Highlight primary target audience (e.g., "E-shops and personal brands in Prague")
- Create separate landing pages for different segments
- Emphasize specific industries in testimonials

#### 2. **CTA Confusion - Too Many Options** üîò
**Problem:** Homepage has 5+ different CTAs:
- "Book appointment"
- "See results"
- "I want one of the spots"
- "Learn more about packages"
- "Start Quiz"

**Consequence:** [Hick's Law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hick%27s_law) - more options = slower decision = fewer conversions.

**Solution:**
- **1 primary CTA** per section: "Book Free Consultation"
- Secondary CTA can be "View Portfolio"
- Move Quiz to lead magnet email sequence

#### 3. **Missing Clear Pricing on Homepage** üí∞
**Problem:** Packages show "Custom pricing" without indicative numbers.

**Consequence:** Client assumes either "too expensive" or "cheap = low quality."

**Solution:**
- Show indicative price range: "from 25,000 CZK"
- Or specific prices for main packages
- The "3 Reels for 15,000 CZK" offer is an excellent example - this should be standard

#### 4. **Navigation Is Overcrowded** üìç
**Current menu:** Packages | Portfolio | References | UGC/Actor | SocialVids | Blog | Quiz | FAQ | About | Contact

**Problem:** 10 menu items = cognitive overload.

**Solution:**
```
Proposed menu (5 items):
- Services (dropdown: Reels Packages, Creative Production, UGC)
- Portfolio
- References
- About
- [BUTTON] Book Now
```

#### 5. **Quiz on Homepage Is a Distraction** üéÆ
**Problem:** Quiz "Do you make these Reels mistakes?" is an engagement tool, not a conversion tool.

**Consequence:** Visitor takes quiz ‚Üí gets result ‚Üí leaves without ordering.

**Solution:**
- Move quiz to separate landing page
- Use as lead magnet (email for results)
- Keep only main offer on homepage
