2. **Carry all equipment** in carry-on (no checked bags)
3. **Have EU insurance certificate** ready to provide
4. **Scout venue day before** event if possible
5. **Build in buffer time** for unexpected delays

1. **Bundle multi-city tours** for better margins
2. **Quote all-inclusive prices** (no surprise fees)
3. **Premium pricing for last-minute** bookings
4. **Loyalty discount** for repeat clients

1. **Target conference organizers** not individual companies
2. **Network at events you attend** (always bring business cards)
3. **Showcase bilingual capabilities** (major differentiator)
4. **Emphasize consistency** across multiple events
