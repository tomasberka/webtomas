Professional Voice For Your Brand

Visuals are only half the story. Voice adds credibility, emotion, and memorability. You may know my voice from campaigns for Plzeňský Prazdroj, ČSOB, and Oreo — and now it can work for you.

Where You'll Hear My Voice

  • TV & online ads — Energetic, confident delivery. For 15-60 second spots. Czech and English
  • E-learning & training — Clear, calm voice for educational materials. Engaging even in hour-long recordings
  • Corporate presentations — Voiceover for brand films, annual reports, internal communications
  • Podcasts & audio content — Hosting, intro/outro, ad reads
  • IVR & phone systems — Professional greetings for corporate phone systems
  • Narration & dubbing — Documentaries, explainer videos, audiobooks

Why Me

I have my own professional recording studio — which means two things: pristine sound quality (no echo, noise, or room tone) and extremely fast turnaround. Most orders delivered within 24 hours.

As an actor with theatre and film training, I control my voice at a different level than a typical 'person with a microphone'. Punchy energy for commercials, calm clarity for e-learning, or character work for dubbing — I adapt to what your brand needs.
